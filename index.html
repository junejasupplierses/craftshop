The Makers

Meet Our Artisans

Passionate craftspeople keeping India's rich handcraft heritage alive

🎨
Mahiras Art Shop
Handmade Art & Crafts
Mahiras Art Shop is a passionate creative studio from the heart of Kutch, bringing together vibrant handmade art pieces, crochet goodies, and unique handcrafted creations that reflect the rich culture of Bhuj.
📍 Bhuj, Kutch, Gujarat
🧵
Zeenat Crafts
Embroidery, Designer Wear & Mud Art
Zeenat Crafts is a versatile creative soul from Bhuj, specialising in exquisite hand embroidery, designer clothes, and embroidered goodies — and also bringing the ancient art of mud art & clay to life. Every creation is deeply rooted in the rich textile and folk traditions of Kutch, where each stitch and mould is a celebration of heritage and craftsmanship.
📍 Bhuj, Kutch, Gujarat
📷
Rehan's Shots
Photography & Visual Storytelling
Rehan's Shots is the lens through which Kutch comes alive. A passionate photographer and visual storyteller, Rehan captures the untold beauty of nature, age-old traditions, local crafts, and the diverse wildlife of the Rann. Beyond photography, he actively supports content creators and filmmakers — offering raw footage, location guidance, and behind-the-scenes collaboration to bring Kutch's stories to the world.
📍 Bhuj, Kutch, Gujarat
